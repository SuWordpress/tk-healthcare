FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between wholesale and retail buying?
Wholesale is for businesses buying in bulk (cartons and above) at significantly discounted prices. Retail is for individuals buying single packs at standard prices. Both are available on our platform — just choose your mode at checkout.
Do I need a prescription to buy medicines?
For over-the-counter (OTC) products, no prescription is needed. For prescription-only medicines (POMs), we require a valid prescription from a licensed healthcare professional. Our pharmacist team can assist you with this requirement.
Are all your products NAFDAC approved?
Yes. Every product in our catalog is NAFDAC-registered and sourced directly from licensed manufacturers or authorized importers. We verify every product batch before stocking.
How do I apply for a wholesale account?
Fill in the application form on our Wholesale page. You'll need your business registration number, PCN license (for pharmacies), and contact details. Our team reviews applications within 24 hours.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, debit/credit cards, USSD payments, and POS on delivery (select areas). Wholesale accounts may qualify for credit terms of 30–60 days.
Can I return products if there's an issue?
Yes. Unopened products may be returned within 7 days of delivery. Damaged, expired, or incorrect products are replaced free of charge within 48 hours of you reporting the issue — no questions asked.
Do you deliver outside Lagos?
Absolutely. We deliver to all 36 states and FCT Abuja. Lagos deliveries take 4–24 hours, major cities 24–48 hours, and the rest of Nigeria 48–72 hours. All orders are fully tracked.

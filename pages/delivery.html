Policies

Delivery policies

📦 Free delivery threshold

Free delivery on retail orders above ₦10,000 and all wholesale orders above ₦50,000. Standard delivery fee applies below threshold.

❌ Failed delivery

If delivery fails due to incorrect address or recipient unavailability, we will reattempt delivery the next business day at no extra charge (once only).

🔄 Returns & refunds

Unopened products may be returned within 7 days. Damaged or incorrect items are replaced free of charge within 48 hours of reporting.

❄️ Cold-chain products

Vaccines, insulin, and other temperature-sensitive products are transported in validated cold-chain packaging with temperature monitoring.

⏰ Delivery windows

Orders placed before 12 PM are dispatched same day. After 12 PM orders are dispatched the next business day. No delivery on Sundays & public holidays.

📱 Real-time tracking

All orders receive an SMS and email with a tracking link once dispatched. You can monitor your delivery status at any time on our website.
